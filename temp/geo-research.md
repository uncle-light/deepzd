# GEO Research Notes

## What is GEO?

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is an emerging practice focused on optimizing digital content to enhance visibility within AI-generated results. Unlike traditional SEO which aims to rank on SERPs, GEO ensures content is cited, referenced, and recommended by AI platforms like ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and Perplexity.

## Key Strategies

### 1. Content Clarity
- Clear, straightforward language
- Avoid jargon
- Use bullet points and short paragraphs
- Highlight key info with bold text

### 2. Direct Answers
- Provide direct answers to common questions
- Include FAQ sections
- Offer actionable advice

### 3. Structured Data
- Implement schema markup
- Help AI extract and index content
- Use semantic HTML

### 4. Authority Building
- Enhance author profiles with credentials
- Include trust signals
- Cite authoritative sources

### 5. E-E-A-T Principles
- Expertise
- Experience  
- Authoritativeness
- Trustworthiness

- 40%+ visibility improvement with GEO optimization
